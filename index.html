FOUR MOVES, THEN GO

Working the Menus

The software's called OnionOS. That's the whole thing in four moves.

🎮 Find & favorite

Games are grouped by console. Tap L/R to flip between Recents, Favorites & each system. A plays, B backs out. Press SELECT on any game to add it to Favorites.

💾 Save anywhere★ THE BIG ONE

Hold MENU+R2 to save a state — a bookmark to the exact moment, droppable anywhere. MENU+L2 loads it back; MENU+←/→ changes slots. Perfect before a boss, or to quit mid-level.

⏯️ Switch & exit

Tap MENU for the GameSwitcher (jump between recent games); double-tap MENU MENU to resume the last one. Hold MENU to save & exit to the menu.

⚡ Handy hotkeys

Hold MENU + a button: R fast-forward · L rewind · SELECT RetroArch menu · POWER screenshot. Tap POWER to sleep & instant-resume.
