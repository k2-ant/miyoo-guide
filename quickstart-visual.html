The Collection — Quick Start

Spider-Man, X-Men, Disney, Pokémon, classics & hundreds more — grab one and press start!

How to play

Find & play L/R lists · A play · B back · SELECT favorite ⭐
Switch / quit tap MENU switcher · hold MENU save & quit
Fast-forward hold MENU+R (skip slow text)
Sleep tap POWER — wakes where you left off
★ Save anywhere! Hold MENU+R2 to bookmark the exact moment; MENU+L2 loads it back — so you never lose your progress.
